Our approach

Thoughtful sourcing

We buy from smallholder farmers and co-ops with transparent pricing. Lots are selected for balance and character, then profiled for sweetness first.

Roasted for clarity

Small-drum, data-logged roasts with consistent curves. Roast dates are printed on every bag and we ship within 24–48 hours for peak aroma.

Sustainability

Compostable bags, recyclable mailers, and carbon-offset shipping. We invest 1% of revenue in producer-led community projects.

Our mission is to make daily rituals warmer—supporting growers, caring for the planet, and helping people brew better at home.
